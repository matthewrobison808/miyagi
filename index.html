A Few Things about Mr Miyagi

This is Mr Miyagi the cat, not the martial arts teacher from "Karate Kid".

What Mr Miyagi Likes

Here are few things that Mr Miyagi likes:

  • Sleep. In order of most preferred sleeping arrangement to the least:
    1. In his cat bowl in front of the heater
    2. On a warm lap
    3. Basking in a ray of warm sunshine
    4. On the back of the couch next to a wide window
  • Eating. Once he was a somewhat picky eater, but now he has broadened his eating horizons to include:
    • Standard Kibble of the organic variety
    • Tuna from his daily tuna water
    • Yogurt
    • Cat grass freshly grown from seed
  • Playing. There is one thing that sums up how Mr Miyagi likes to play; diversity. Every skillset needs a different toy if he is to become a well trained master.
    • "Sexy Girlfriend" trains Miyagi how to burrow under a rug and fish for critters
    • "Exotica" trains him on how to leap into the air and perform backflips and other aerial manuvers.
    • Blue tinsle balls train him on accuracy. How to leap from a hiding place and pounce on his prey.

Other Skills of Mr Miyagi

No cat should be limited to number of tricks they can learn. This is why Mr Miyagi uses the toilet instead of the litter box to go to the bathroom.
